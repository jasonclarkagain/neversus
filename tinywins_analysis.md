# TinyWins.com Analysis

## Brand Identity
- **Tagline**: "Crafting Brands that Inspire Irrational Love"
- **Subtitle**: "TinyWins is a Global Feelings Studio"
- **Description**: "We build brands that triumph with emotion"
- **Ambition**: "Irrational LOVE for your brand"

## Design Style
- Dark/black background on hero section
- Bold, massive typography (TINYWINS in huge white letters)
- Minimal, editorial aesthetic
- Black and white with accent colors (green used in some elements)
- Clean, modern sans-serif fonts
- Horizontal scrolling news ticker for recent wins/partners
- Full-bleed imagery for case studies

## Navigation
- WORK | ABOUT | SERVICES | HUGE VICTORIES | CAREERS | TALK TO US

## Sections
1. **Hero** - Giant logo + tagline + subtitle
2. **Recent Wins Ticker** - Horizontal scrolling list of new challenges/partners
3. **Featured Case Study** - Full-width image with overlay text (Joby)
4. **Ambition Statement** - "Irrational LOVE for your brand"
5. **Studio Reel** - Video section
6. **Services** - Listed as:
   - Future Proofing & Emerging Tech (01)
   - Marketing & Creative Services (02)
   - Digital Design & Experience (03)
   - Brand Strategy & Identity (04)
7. **Work Portfolio** - Grid of case studies with "Feeling [emotion]" labels
8. **News/Press** - Recent press mentions and partner news
9. **Footer** - Full links, social media, contact

## Key Features
- Horizontal scrolling ticker for recent work/partners
- Case study cards with emotional descriptors ("Feeling optimized", "Feeling radiant")
- Studio reel video
- Press/news section
- Numbered services list

## Color Palette
- Primary: Black (#000000)
- Secondary: White (#FFFFFF)
- Accent: Bright green (used in UI elements)
- Typography: Bold, condensed sans-serif

## What to Improve
1. Better color palette with more vibrancy
2. More interactive animations and micro-interactions
3. Smoother scrolling experience
4. Better mobile responsiveness
5. More engaging hero section
6. Cleaner portfolio grid layout
7. Better testimonials/social proof section
8. More personality in the copy
9. Faster load times
10. Better accessibility
